Chocolate Chip Cookies

Video credit: The Cooking Foodie on YouTube

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugars with a mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the salt, vanilla, and eggs, and beat until well mixed.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture, and mix until just combined.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop heaping tablespoon-size balls of dough about 2 inches apart on the baking sheets.
  8. Bake until cookies are golden around the edges but still soft in the center, 8 to 10 minutes.
  9. Let cookies cool on the baking sheets for 1-2 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips

  • For chewier cookies, chill the dough for at least 30 minutes before baking.
  • Add a pinch of cinnamon for a unique twist.
  • Use a mix of different chocolate chips for more variety in flavor.

Lemon Drizzle Cake

Ingredients

  • 225g unsalted butter, softened
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 100g icing sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (for drizzle)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180째C (350째F). Grease and line a loaf tin.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, until fully incorporated.
  4. Fold in the flour and lemon zest until well combined.
  5. Spoon the mixture into the prepared loaf tin and level the surface.
  6. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Mix the icing sugar and lemon juice to make the drizzle.
  8. While the cake is still warm, prick it all over with a fork and pour over the drizzle.
  9. Let the cake cool completely in the tin before removing and serving.

Tips

  • Add some poppy seeds to the batter for extra texture.
  • Serve with a dollop of whipped cream for an indulgent treat.
  • Store in an airtight container to keep the cake moist.
